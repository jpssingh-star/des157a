Dance Battle!

It’s Snoopy vs Courage on the dance floor!

How to Play:

  • Find a friend and fight over which character you want!
  • Press START to see who opens the dance-off.
  • When it’s your character’s turn, click the Character's turn button to perform a dance move
  • Watch the moves and see how your opponent reacts:
    • No reaction = full hype
    • Small reaction = half hype
    • Block completely = no hype
  • Keep dancing until one character reaches 100% hype and wins!

Tips:

  • Each attack is random, no two battles are the same
  • Keep an eye on the hype bars, they show who’s dominating the dance floor
  • Have fun and watch their groovy moves!
